About Me

Louis T. Delia is a musician, writer, business professional, and freelance web developer. He currently divides his time between coding, blogging, and writing music. Louis graduated cum laude from The College of New Jersey, where he studied Interdisciplinary Business and Music. He now studies Web Development at Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. He currently resides in Edison, New Jersey.
